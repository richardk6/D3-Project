Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Income

Many magazines print opinion pieces on the best state to live in related to many different attributes in our lives. As a research team, we decided to pull the data and compare with state data to see if there is any truth in these pieces.

We pulled the many different attributes for each state, which you can click through on the scatter chart. You can see many southern states are not as healthy and wealthy as many northern states. I think more articles need to stop celebrating the health of states with more white people and work to bring more attention to the lack of healthcare and livable income in these states.
